    Dynamic Island

    Dynamic Island.
    For when things just pop up.

    Dynamic Island bubbles up alerts and Live Activities — so you don’t miss them while you’re doing something else. You can track your next ride, see who’s calling, check your flight status, and so much more.

    Dynamic Island. For when

    things just pop up.

    When something bubbles up, just touch and hold to act on it. Dynamic Island expands so you can find out when your dinner will arrive, rewind your podcast or see if your AirPods have connected and their battery level.

    Double bubble.

    If two activities are happening at once, Dynamic Island splits in two. So now you can chat with a friend and keep an eye on your timer. Or see what song is playing while you check your running pace. Just touch and hold to expand one of the activities, or tap to open the app.

    All kinds of activities. And expanding.

    Dynamic Island can let you know if your flight is delayed, track your hike and tell you when your workout will end. And new apps are added all the time, so it keeps getting more and more helpful.

    AirPods charging, Tide Guide, Flighty, Citymapper, CARROT Weather, Alarm, Active call.

    A display that shines in bright sunlight.

    The Super Retina XDR display is up to twice as bright in the sun compared to iPhone 14, so you’ll really notice the difference. And a big boost in peak HDR brightness makes movies — and even the videos you take — more brilliant.

    StandBy for
    more
    information.

    StandBy2 turns your iPhone into a new full-screen experience with glanceable information you can see from a distance. Just set it on its side while charging to display the time, showcase your favourite photos or keep an eye on your widgets.

    48MP

    Now the Main camera shoots in super-high resolution. So it’s easier than ever to take standout photos with amazing detail — from snapshots to stunning landscapes.

    All‑new 48MP Main camera.
    For breathtaking, smile-making picture taking.

    More room to zoom.

    Three optical-quality zoom levels let you effortlessly frame the perfect shot from right where you are.

    Choose 2x Telephoto to zoom in for that perfect close‑up.

    Choose 1x for detailed middle‑distance shots.

    Choose 0.5x to capture way more of the scene with the Ultra Wide camera.

    Night mode

    Night mode automatically activates in especially dark scenes and now captures even richer colour and sharper detail.

    Smart HDR

    When lighting is bright or uneven, the latest Smart HDR intelligently optimises for both your subject and the background. So you get gorgeous colour and even more true-to-life skin tones.

    A front camera auto-focused on you.

    The TrueDepth camera automatically focuses on you and your friends for spectacular selfies and super-sharp FaceTime calls.

    Videos that deserve the most views.

    Take 4K HDR videos with amazing detail, colour and contrast. Use Action mode to steady a really shaky handheld shot. Or try 4K Cinematic mode to automatically shift the focus to the most important subject in a scene.

    Use Action mode for smooth handheld videos

    Portraits

    Focus‑pocus, magical new portraits.

    More detail and richer colour make a dramatic difference
    in your portraits. And now you can tap to shift the
    focus between subjects — even after you take the shot    .
    Presto chango.

    Want to quickly capture the moment before it’s gone? You no longer have to switch to Portrait mode. If your subject is a person, dog or cat, iPhone 15 automatically captures depth information. So you can choose to instantly see your photo as a portrait, with an artful blur effect. Or later in the Photos app.

    Focus-pocus, magical new portraits.

    Keep on zooming.

    Use the optical-quality 2x Telephoto to zoom in and frame the perfect portrait from right where you are. Need to go closer still? Use continuous zoom to move in even more.

    Phantastic Photonic photos.

    The new Photonic Engine supercharges your portraits. It combines the best pixels from a super‑high‑resolution image with another that’s optimised for light capture.
    So you get a 24MP image with twice the resolution. It’s perfect for everyday photos — at a file size small enough for storing and sharing.

    A16 Bionic chip

    A16 Bionic chip. A Pro-proven powerhouse.

    A16 Bionic powers all kinds of advanced features. Like computational photography used for 24MP photos and next-generation portraits. Voice Isolation for phone calls. And smooth performance for graphics-intensive games. All with incredible efficiency for great battery life. No wonder it started as a Pro chip.

    The A16 Bionic GPU is up to 40% faster than the GPU in iPhone 12

    Connectivity

    USB-Compatible.

    The new USB-C connector lets you charge your Mac or iPad with the same cable you use to charge iPhone 15. You can even use iPhone 15 to charge Apple Watch or AirPods.5 Bye-bye, cable clutter.

    USB-Compatible.

    Turbocharge.

    Charge up to 50% in around 30 minutes6 with the 20W USB-C Power Adapter. Or add a MagSafe Charger for fast, efficient wireless charging. (Both are sold separately.)

    Ah, there you are.

    Want to find a friend in a crowded train station or busy park? Look no further than Precision Finding for Find My friends. It’s powered by the second-generation Ultra Wideband chip,7 now with three times greater range, so it can lead you right to them.

    To enable Precision Finding, friends with iPhone 15 Pro or iPhone 15 can share their location only with you using Find My. It’s simple — and private.

    Crash

    Detection.

    iPhone can detect a severe car crash and call for help if you can’t.10

    Hardware sensors and advanced motion algorithms identify signs of a crash, like sudden shifts in speed and direction, or changes in cabin pressure caused by deploying airbags.

    Then iPhone can call emergency services and notify your emergency contacts.

    Over 1 million hours of real-world driving and crash data helps iPhone recognise accidents.

    Values

    Designed to make a difference.

    What matters to you matters to Apple too. From privacy protections that give you more control over your data. To using more recycled materials that minimise environmental impact. To creating built-in features that make iPhone accessible to all.

    Designed to make
    a difference.

    Privacy. That’s iPhone.

    The Health app makes it easy for you to access your health and fitness information — and securely share access with apps, relatives and doctors.

    Passkeys eliminate the need for passwords on apps and websites by using unique digital keys. Just use Touch ID or Face ID to authenticate. Simple and secure.

    Safari Enhanced Private Browsing locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading and removes tracking URLs.

    More on Apple and Privacy

    Big steps toward a smaller footprint.

    iPhone 15 contains more recycled materials than ever:

    100% recycled cobalt in the battery11

    100% recycled copper foil in the MagSafe inductive charger

    Now 99% of iPhone packaging is fibre based,12 with 100% recycled or responsibly sourced wood fibres.13 And there’s still no plastic wrap around the box.

    Our stores, offices and data centres are already carbon neutral. By 2030, our products — and your carbon footprint from using them — will be too.

    More on Apple and the Environment

    iPhone for all.

    If you’re at risk of losing your ability to speak, Personal Voice lets you create a voice that sounds like you, so you can communicate with it using Live Speech for calls and conversations.

    Assistive Access helps users with cognitive disabilities tailor apps and experiences. For example, Messages can offer visual communication tools, like video messages,
    and the Home Screen can emphasise large text and icons.

    VoiceOver is a screen reader that tells people who are blind or have low vision exactly what’s happening on iPhone. It can describe friends, objects, text, photos and even graphs in great detail.
